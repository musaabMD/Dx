# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-27

99recipe ("we", "us", "our") provides a nutrition and meal-tracking application.

## Information we collect

- Account information from authentication providers (for example, user ID and profile metadata).
- User-provided nutrition and meal data (meal entries, preferences, and goals).
- Basic technical diagnostics for reliability and security.

## How we use information

- To provide core app functionality.
- To sync your account and meal data.
- To improve app reliability and prevent abuse.

## Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with service providers required to operate the app (for example, authentication and backend infrastructure).

## Data retention

We retain data while your account is active and as needed for legal and operational purposes.

## Your choices

- You can request account and data deletion by contacting support.
- You can update profile fields in-app where available.
