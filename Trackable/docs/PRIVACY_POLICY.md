# Trackable Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Trackable is designed as a private, on-device goal tracking app. The app does not require a username or password.

## Data We Use

Trackable can read data only after you grant permission, including:

- Apple Health data for goals such as steps, walking distance, active calories, workouts, sleep, and heart rate.
- Reminders permission for completed-reminder goals.
- Location permission for optional home/outside goals.
- Device information such as battery level and available storage.

## Data Storage

Your goals, targets, progress summaries, settings, and subscription cache are stored locally on your device using Apple frameworks.

## Data Sharing

Trackable does not sell personal data. Trackable does not include third-party advertising SDKs or third-party analytics SDKs in this app version.

## Purchases

If you buy Trackable Pro, purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. Trackable reads Apple transaction entitlement status to unlock paid features.

## Deleting Data

You can delete app data inside the app from Settings > Data > Delete all app data. You can also remove the app from your device.

## Contact

For privacy requests or support, contact: support@example.com
