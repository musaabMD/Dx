132. Aortic stenosis patient for extraction → antibiotic prophylaxis?  
* ​​According to these guidelines, antibiotic prophylaxis is indicated only for certain cardiac conditions with the highest risk of infective endocarditis. These include prosthetic heart valves or prosthetic material used for cardiac valve repair, previous infective endocarditis, unrepaired cyanotic congenital heart disease (CHD), completely repaired congenital heart defect with prosthetic material, or CHD with residual defect at the site or adjacent to the site of a prosthetic patch, and cardiac transplant patients who develop cardiac valvulopathy.
